Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

How do I place an order on AgriShop?
Placing an order on AgriShop is simple. Browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to provide your shipping information and payment details to complete the order.
Do you offer organic products?
Yes, we offer a wide range of organic products including seeds, fertilizers, and pesticides. All our organic products are certified and meet the highest standards of quality.
How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. We also offer express delivery options for urgent orders. Delivery times may vary based on your location and product availability.
Can I rent farming equipment from AgriShop?
Yes, we offer a farming equipment rental service. You can browse our available tools and equipment, select the ones you need, and rent them for the required duration. This is especially useful for expensive equipment that you may only need occasionally.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 7-day return policy for most products. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it in its original packaging for a full refund or exchange. Some restrictions may apply to certain products.

About AgriShop

In the fertile lands of Pune, Maharashtra, AgriShop was born in 2010 not merely as a business, but as a response to a pressing need in India's agricultural landscape. Our founder, Mr. Hitesh Lokare, an agricultural scientist with over two decades of experience, witnessed firsthand the challenges farmers faced in accessing quality agricultural products, reliable information, and modern farming tools. What started as a small storefront with just 50 essential products and a passionate team of three individuals has blossomed into India's most trusted agricultural marketplace, today serving over 50,000 farmers across the length and breadth of the nation. Our journey mirrors the growth of Indian agriculture itself—evolving from traditional practices to embrace innovation while staying rooted in the timeless wisdom of farming communities. Each milestone in our growth story has been driven by one fundamental principle: putting the farmer first. From introducing our revolutionary tool rental program that made expensive equipment accessible to small farmers, to launching our AI-powered crop disease detection platform that brings cutting-edge technology to rural fields, every innovation at AgriShop has been designed to solve real problems faced by real farmers.

we've been serving the agricultural community for over a decade. Our team consists of agriculture experts who carefully select and test every product we offer to ensure it meets our high standards of quality and effectiveness.

Our Mission

At AgriShop, our fundamental mission is to empower every Indian farmer with comprehensive access to world-class agricultural products, cutting-edge technology, and transformative knowledge while steadfastly fostering sustainable farming practices that ensure food security, economic prosperity, and environmental stewardship for current and future generations. We are driven by the unwavering belief that quality agricultural inputs should not be a luxury but a fundamental right for every farmer, regardless of their land size or location, which is why we work relentlessly to democratize access through our extensive distribution network, affordable pricing strategies, and innovative tool rental programs that break down financial barriers to modern farming equipment. Our mission extends beyond mere product provision to encompass holistic farmer empowerment through rigorous quality assurance protocols that guarantee every seed, fertilizer, and tool meets the highest standards of performance and reliability, coupled with comprehensive knowledge dissemination initiatives including free workshops, expert consultations, and digital learning platforms that translate complex agricultural research into practical, actionable insights for farmers in the field. We are deeply committed to leading India's transition toward sustainable agriculture by promoting eco-friendly farming practices, water conservation techniques, and organic alternatives that protect our environment while enhancing productivity and profitability for farming communities
